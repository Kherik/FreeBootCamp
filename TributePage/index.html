J.R.R. Tolkein

The man who inspired generations of fantasy nerds.

J.R.R Tolkien was the original master world builder, creating incredibly detailed factions, races, magic, mythos and languages. He inspired generations of fictional fantasy gurus.

Tribute Info

  • J.R.R. Tolkien was a master linguist, he manipulated all of the languages the he knew from the real world and create his entire Elven language as well as pieces of the Dwarven language
